

### 2.1

Consider the context-free grammar:

S -> S S + | S S * | a

1. Show how the string `aa+a*` can be generated by this grammar.

aa+a* -> [aa+][a]*

2. Construct a parse tree for this string.


aa+a*
|
+- aa+
|	+- a
|	+- a
|	+- +
+- a
+- *

3. What language does this grammar generate? Justify your answer.
